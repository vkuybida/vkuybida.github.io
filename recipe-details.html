Ingredients
  • 1 package McCain® Tasti Taters®
  • 1 can (284ml) condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/2 Cup of milk
  • 1/2 Cup of sour cream
Preparation
  • Preheat the oven to 350F (176C)
  • Mix the soup, milk and sour cream in a bowl.
  • In a baking dish (9 x13) spread melted butter.
  • Spread half of the McCain® Tasti Taters® evenly in the dish.
