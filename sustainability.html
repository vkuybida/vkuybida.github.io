Sustainability

AT MCCAIN, WE ARE ON A MISSION TO MAKE

DELICIOUS, PLANET-FRIENDLY FOOD

Investing in Smart &
Sustainable Farming

Good things start with soil. We’re committed to sustainable agriculture practices that help improve soil health. Because how we grow food makes a huge difference to the health of our planet.

By 2023: We Will Source 100% of Our Potatoes from Farms Practicing Regenerative Agriculture.

We have developed a framework for our farmers to support their progression toward more regenerative agriculture practices. This guide, developed in consultation with academia, scientific organizations, farmer representatives, public institutions, customers and NGOs – is available here.

The Future Starts in
FLORENCEVILLE

We’ve created a State-of-the-Art “Farm of the Future” in Florenceville, New Brunswick, where we test innovative techniques that allow us to better understand regenerative agricultural practices and their impact, costs and benefits.

Then, we share the knowledge with farmers, inviting them to come and see our developments, helping them farm more sustainably for future generations.

Supporting Communities
Coast to Coast

At McCain, we love to give back. In fact, every year our employees give back over

2,000 volunteer hours

rom fundraising to food banks – in the communities we serve across Canada.

Additionally, in partnership with Second Harvest McCain helps fight hunger in Canada by donating more than half a Million meals to those in need every year.

In 2023 we were honoured to receive the Second Harvest Values Award for showcasing innovation, visionary action, and corporate culture as we tackle hunger and food waste.

Reducing our
Climate Impact

Making more, using LESS of the Earth’s Resources

Recyclable Packaging

We’re proud to say that all of our Canadian packaging is 100% recyclable

Renewable Energy

Globally, McCain is committed to running on 100% renewable electricity by 2030

Reducing Emissions

Globally, we have set a goal to cut carbon emissions in half by 2030, consistent with the ambitious goals of the Paris Agreement to keep global warming to 1.5°C.

We are the first food and beverage company in Canada to be approved and vetted by the Science Based Targets Initiative (SBTI) for its reduction plan!

As a global leader in prepared food products, we believe that we have the responsibility to drive positive change for our business, our communities and our planet.
